Traffic Jam Simulation

Date Created: 1/17/2021

Code: Github

If you've ever been driving on the highway in traffic then, all of the sudden, it clears up, this is a simulation showcasing why that happened.

If drivers only focus on the car in front of them to avoid a collision, then the tiniest decrease in one speed causes the car behind them to brake to a little slower, then repeat for the next car and the next and so on. This happens until eventually one car stops completely.

A way to prevent this is to not only look at the car in front of you but also behind you and try to move to the halfway point.
