Planetary Distances

Date Created: 2/10/2020

Which planet is closest to the Earth? This seems like a simple question but as can be seen, its not so cut and dry. Depending on where each planet is in it's orbit, one planet might be closest or another.

Here's a better question. Which planet is closest to the Earth most of the time? You might think its Mars or perhaps Venus but, strangely, the answer is Mercury

Even more strangely, Mercury is the closest object, on average, to everything that orbits around the Sun
