Boids

Date Created: 6/13/2020

Boids is an algorithm that simulates the flocking behavior of bids. By using a few simple rules for each boid to follow, flocking behavior emerges.

One of the rules is alignment, which tries to steer toward the average heading of nearby boids. Another is separation which tries to steer away from other boids that are too close. The last key rule is cohesion which is to try to move to the average position of nearby boids.
