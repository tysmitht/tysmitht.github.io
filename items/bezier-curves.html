Bezier Curves

Date Created: 8/3/2020

Code: Github

Bezier curves are most commonly used in computer graphics To generate smooth, arbitrary curves. They are great for computers because a single bezier curve can be described by less than 10 integers.

Typically, you see Bezier curves represented by a system of equations which is better for computation and actually using a Bezier curve in the real world. However, this method was my first introduction to Bezier curves.
